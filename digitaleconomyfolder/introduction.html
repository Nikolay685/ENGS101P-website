Digital Economy

Introduction
Digital economy refers to an economy system in which the wide use of digital computing technology brings change in the entire economic environment and economic activity. Digital economy is also a new social, politic, and economic system in which all the information and business activities are digitalized. Online trades among enterprise, consumer and government grow rapidly. Main researche s in digital economy involve commodities and services that are dependent on digital computing technology in their production, distribution and selling.¹
