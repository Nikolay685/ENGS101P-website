Practical appllications of digital economy


Online shop: With help of digital technology sellers can post the information of their products on the internet and consumers can choose the commodities by categorizing the information of products online.


Online payment: It is the payment channel between the bank and the customer provided by the third party. People can directly transfer their payment to the other online bank account without manual confirmation. Thus the online payment improves efficiency of transactions.


Transport: The management of transport network can be easy as data about transportation tools, commodities, and working staffs will be updated and obtained from internet duing the process of transportation.
