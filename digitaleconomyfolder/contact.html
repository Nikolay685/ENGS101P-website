Impact to society


Digital economy brings higher working efficiency because capital and human resources can be better allocated. Also, the arise of businesses, such as shopping online, social networking tool and game online, offers more job vacancies.

Digital economy promotes the globalization of economy as transactions between countries happen more frequently. Customers nowadays demand for world-wide products and digital technology plays a significant part in global transaction.(5)

The difference between consumers and producers become smaller as involvement of consumer in the process of actual production. Consumers are able to choose or even design the product they themselves prefer, such as the special ordered commodities online.(5)
