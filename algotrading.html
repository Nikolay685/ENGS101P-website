Algorithmic Trading

What is Algorithmic Trading?

Algorithmic Trading is a trading system that uses complex mathematical models and algorithms to make quick decisions and transactions in the financial markets. Algorithmic Trading uses fast computers and complex formulas to determine best trading strategy for current and future market positions.

Benefits of Algorithmic Trading

  • Traders executed at the best possible prices
  • Instant and accurate trade order placement
  • Reduced transaction costs
  • Reduced risk of manual errors in placing the trades
  • Simultaneous automated checks on multiple market conditions

Some Trading Strategies

Trading-Following

Simple strategy where you simply follow market trends. The buy and sell orders generate based on the instructions set by technical indicators. This strategy is also able to compare historical data with current to help with the predictions.

Mean Reversion

The basics of this strategy is it calculates an average asset price using historical data and takes trades of the current price returning the average price.

News-Based

In this strategy, the trading software is hooked to news wires and automatically generates trade signals depending on how actual data turns out in comparison to the market.

Arbitrage

Using arbitrage in algorithmic trading basically means that the software hunts for price imbalances across different markets and makes profit of those.
